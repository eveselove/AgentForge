## 5. Rollback / Safety Notes

- Disable: `AGENTFORGE_RUST_FLYWHEEL=0` in services + `export` in workers, or remove `source` of snippet + restart.
- Rate limit can be raised (e.g. EVERY_N=20) or set to 1 for aggressive (use with care on busy farm).
- All hooks are non-blocking + best-effort (never fail a task).
- Binary guard prevents accidental heavy runs if you haven't built `agentforge-runner`.
- Counters live in `/tmp` (reset on reboot or `rm -f /tmp/agentforge_rust_flywheel/.flywheel_*`).
